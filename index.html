Why Coron?

Coron is world-renowned for its wreck diving sites

Coron, Palawan is a stunning destination in the Philippines, renowned for its natural beauty and outdoor activities. It is famous for its breathtaking lakes, such as Kayangan Lake and Barracuda Lake, known for their crystal-clear waters and dramatic limestone cliffs. The waters around Coron are teeming with vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life, making it a paradise for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts.

Coron is world-renowned for its wreck diving sites, with several Japanese shipwrecks from World War II resting in its waters. Mount Tapyas is a popular hiking spot that rewards climbers with a panoramic view of Coron Island and its surroundings. It is a must-visit destination for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and anyone looking to experience the pristine beauty of the Philippines.
